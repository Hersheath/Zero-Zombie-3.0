Zero Zombie

A terrible zombie virus broke out in B city, and the air force were dispatched to clean it up. Try your best, don't let the zombies escape.

You can use the Arrow Keys or the Cursor (using cursor is much easier) to control the plane and press Spacebar to drop the bomb.

Note: Not only the hailstone can damage your plane, zombie can also crash it.

There are also airdrops to collect for ammo and HP.
